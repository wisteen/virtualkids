About Virtuakids

Empowering African kids with digital skills.

Virtuakids Incorporated is an edtech company based in Lagos, Nigeria, created to help pupils and students in primary and secondary schools acquire in-demand digital skills before they leave high school.

Through the Digital Literacy Skill for the African Child (D.L.A.C.) initiative, we aim to equip at least 1,000 children every year with skills that prepare them for the global digital economy.

What Your Kids Will Learn

Our programs are practical, project-based and designed to open kids up to the world of technology:

  • Graphics Design
  • Coding & Web Development
  • UI/UX Design
  • Scratch Programming
  • Robotics
  • Social Media Management

Kids can start building portfolios and even earning while learning.

Why Virtuakids?

  • Real-world, project-based learning: All courses are built around real projects, not just theory.
  • Seasoned instructors: Experienced trainers guide and mentor your kids to become the best in their chosen path.
  • Designed for schools: Sessions run once in a learning week so core academic activities are not disrupted.
  • Values and character: We emphasise responsibility, creativity, problem-solving and nation-building.
  • Early advantage: Students can start earning from their skills and positioning themselves for future careers.

Our Training Sessions

Our training has been meticulously designed by skilled instructors to fit into each schools timetable. It is practical, interactive, problem-solving and eye-opening.

  • • Training runs once in a learning week to avoid disrupting core academics.
  • • All sessions are project-based so kids build real, portfolio-ready work.
  • • Students take a Creative Intelligence Test at the end of each program.

Certificates of completion are issued to pupils and students who complete the curriculum and pass the Creative Intelligence Test for each course.

Tools for the Training

To get the best out of each session, we recommend the following tools:

  • 1. A laptop (64-bit Linux, Windows or Mac) with at least 4GB RAM and 500GB storage.
  • 2. A tablet for coding and graphics, especially for Primary and Junior Secondary classes.
  • 3. An Android-enabled smartphone (optional for those without laptops/tablets).
  • 4. A Designpad from Virtuakids and a mathematical set with all basic tools.

Beyond Skills: Mentorship & Values

Our mentorship program helps your kids grow up to become responsible adults, dependable and skill-oriented. We encourage them to play a major role in nation-building, rather than indulge in social vices and fraud.

We focus on building character, discipline and a mindset of creativity, excellence and service.

Fun, Productivity & Rewards

Learning at Virtuakids is fun as well as productive. As part of our core objective to reward learning, gift prizes are given to the best performing pupils and students for every session.

This keeps learners motivated, engaged and eager to keep improving their skills.
